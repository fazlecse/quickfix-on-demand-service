our process

A Seamless, End-to-End Garment Sourcing Workflow

Requirement Analysis

We begin by understanding product needs- Includes Styling, Yarn types, Fabrication, Embelishments, Types of Trims & Accessories, quantities, timelines, target prices & technical specifications. This ensures alignment from day one.

Vendor Selection & Costing

We match your requirements with the right factory from our trusted network. We negotiate competitive pricing while ensuring compliance, quality, and capacity.

Product Development & Sampling

We develop & deliver required samples based on your tech packs, inspirations, sample comments, revisions & improvements until you’re fully satisfied.

Order Placement & Planning

Once samples are approved, we confirm orders and set a detailed production Timeline. Our Concern teams ensure all raw materials are sourced & in-housed on time to ensure smooth production.

Logistics & Shipping Support

We coordinate logistics, documentation, and shipping — ensuring timely and hassle-free delivery to your destination, whether by sea or air.
